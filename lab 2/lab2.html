Monkey Showcase

The capuchin monkey is a smart, social primate from Central and South America, known for its curiosity, agility, and ability to use tools.

The gibbon is a small, agile ape from Southeast Asia, famous for its long arms, loud calls, and graceful swinging through the trees.

The orangutan is a large, intelligent ape from Southeast Asia, known for its reddish fur, tree-dwelling lifestyle, and remarkable problem-solving abilities.

The chimpanzee is a highly intelligent ape from Africa, known for its complex social groups, tool use, and close genetic link to humans.

The gorilla is the largest ape, native to Africa, and is known for its strength, gentle nature, and tight-knit family groups led by a silverback male.

The spider monkey is a slender, long-limbed primate from Central and South America, known for its prehensile tail and swift movement through the treetops.
